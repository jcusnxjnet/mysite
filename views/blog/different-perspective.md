title: Different perspective
date: 2024-08-29
---
There's nothing like a loved one giving you a fresh perspective. Once, I quit my job and stayed home for eight months. I love telling everyone about how much work I got done on the house, the trips we took as a family, all the books I read, and how it was all just great. Recently, my son said to me, “You mean when you quit your job and then played Minesweeper at home for eight months?”
